# Module 2 Risk Identification

## Introduction

Canada Life's acquisition of Santé Circle Health presents opportunities for growth but also introduces competitive risks that require monitoring.

## Risk 1 – Negative Risk

### Problem Statement

Canada Life faces increasing competition from digital healthcare providers and telehealth companies. If Canada Life does not respond effectively, it may lose customers and experience slower growth.

### Risk Statement

The risk of losing market share caused by increased competition from digital healthcare providers and telehealth companies resulting in reduced customer acquisition and slower revenue growth.
